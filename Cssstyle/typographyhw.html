Football

It is a ball based game between two teams of 11 players. there will be one ball in the center of the feild 

length between 100-130 yards (90-120m) and a
             width of 50-100 yards (45-90m)
There are two goalposts at each end of the field,they both have nets and there are two goalkeepers. Each team has to score by taking the ball to the goalposts and kicking the ball into the goalposts. it is counted by scoring goals.While the opponent team tries to prevent it.
If some one fouls the other player he gets a yellow card and if he gets two then he gets a red card and gets dissqualified from the game.

This is the basic introduction to football
